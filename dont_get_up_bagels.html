Our Story

The Best Bagels on the East Coast. Now at Your Door.

We've partnered exclusively with Spread Bagelry — one of the most celebrated bagel shops on the East Coast — to bring their legendary hand-rolled, wood-fired bagels directly to the Jersey Shore.

Every Saturday, we load up and hit the road so you can wake up to something extraordinary without setting foot in a line.

  • Genuine Spread Bagelry bagels, baked fresh
  • Premium smoked fish, deli meats & artisan spreads
  • Priced lower than waiting at a bagel shop
  • Order by Thursday night, delivered Saturday morning
